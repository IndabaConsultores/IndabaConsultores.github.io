Indaba, as data controller, will process your Personal Data for the purpose of managing the process of maintaining a commercial relationship and receiving information by any means of communication about our products and services.

The legal basis for the processing is based on the consent of the data subject, via the web form.

The data subject has the right to withdraw his or her consent at any time, whereby the provisions on withdrawal of consent in section 9 below apply.

The personal data provided will be kept for the period necessary for the execution of the purposes described in this policy, and as long as the deletion or revocation of consent is not requested by them, in which case, the personal data will be kept blocked for the period necessary to comply with a legal obligation or for the formulation, exercise and defence of claims that may arise from the processing of the same.

Indaba has adopted and will adopt all the technical and organisational security measures that are mandatory in accordance with the provisions of current legislation and the quality standards existing in the sector, in order to guarantee the maximum security and confidentiality of communications. Indaba undertakes to respect the confidentiality of your Personal Data and to guarantee you the exercise of your rights.

Interested parties have the right to access their personal data as well as to request the rectification of inaccurate data or, where appropriate, its deletion, when, among other reasons, the data is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

In certain circumstances and for reasons related to the particular situation of the persons concerned, they may object to the processing of their data. Indaba will stop processing your data except in those cases where this is not possible for compelling legitimate reasons or for the exercise or defence of possible claims.

Due to the characteristics of the service, the right of portability is limited to the data provided, but it may be used.

To exercise these rights you can contact us at the address, email or telephone number indicated above and within a maximum of 48 hours we will indicate the steps to follow and will carry out your request.

In the event that you are not satisfied with the exercise of your rights (or the way to contact us to carry them out) you can file a complaint with the competent Data Protection Control Authority, in this case the Spanish Data Protection Agency.

10. Updating and communication channels

Finally, Indaba undertakes to inform the data subject at all times if in any case the data is processed in a different or new way to those mentioned in section 4 of this Privacy Policy, provided that the consent of the data subject is in force.

The channels of communication will be those used for the collection of data or others that are considered to be similar or equivalent.
